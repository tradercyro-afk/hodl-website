ABOUT HODL

On December 18, 2013, during one of Bitcoin's earliest violent market swings, a frustrated trader posted a now-legendary message titled "I AM HODLING." It was a drunken typo - but the message behind it was crystal clear.

While markets crashed and panic spread, he chose conviction over fear.

HODL became the rallying cry of those who refused to sell the future for short-term comfort. A declaration that time, patience, and conviction would outlast volatility.

HODL became the shared language of long-term believers, a reminder that the strongest position is often the one that doesn't move. Through every cycle, the principle stayed intact: patience beats panic.
